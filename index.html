You can edit your current profile and make a new one by clicking the profile icon.

If you do not select a question category or difficulty, then the questions can be from any category and of any difficulty.

You can choose between challenger mode, where you have five seconds to respond to the question, and normal mode, where you have ten seconds.

The response is deemed incorrect if the specified time expires.

You can only be included in the leaderboard if you have answered 50 questions.

Both the home screen and the screen after the quiz have a leaderboard button.
